Welcome

The robot below simulates a (rather rigid) clean language coach.

Type your responses to its questions in the input space and then press enter.

Alternatively, press the microphone icon in the bottom right of the screen to activate dictation.

For the best results speak plainly and clearly, avoid contractions and too much punctuation.

The coach works entirely in your browser, meaning your input is never sent or stored anywhere.
