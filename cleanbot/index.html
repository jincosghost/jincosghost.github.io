Welcome & Disclaimer

The robot below simulates a (rather rigid) clean language coach.

CleanBot is for entertainment purposes only. Cleanbot only provides a simulation of a clean language coaching session, it does not provide, and is not a substitute for medical, psychological, or psychiatric advice or diagnosis. Always consult a trained medical professional.

Type your responses to its questions in the input space and then press enter. Alternatively, press the microphone icon to activate dictation.

For the best results speak plainly and clearly, avoid contractions and too much punctuation.

The coach works entirely in your browser, meaning your input is never sent or stored anywhere.
